Hello!

I am a 3rd-year astronomy PhD student at the University of Texas at Austin. My research interests are on the theory side of cosmology, reionization, 21cm signal, and line intensity mapping.

On this website you will find projects I worked on in both grad school and undergrad, and some neat visualizations. In undergrad I worked on the persistent homology of the cosmic web and on the evolution of large-scale structure using Lagrangian catastrophe theory. You will also find pages dedicated to some of my other interests.
